By downloading or using LuminaCore, you agree to these terms. The app is distributed through the Microsoft Store and is also subject to Microsoft's standard terms of use.

What the App Does

LuminaCore monitors your PC's real-time power consumption using LibreHardwareMonitor, which reads actual hardware sensor data (CPU, GPU, RAM). It calculates electricity cost projections using a unit price you configure. All processing happens locally on your PC.

About Accuracy

Power values are based on hardware sensor readings where available, combined with statistical estimation for components without direct sensor support. Real consumption may vary depending on your hardware configuration, workload, and environment. Use the figures as a practical guide.

System Requirements

LuminaCore requires Windows 10 or Windows 11 (x64) and .NET 8 runtime (bundled with the app). Administrator permissions may be required for hardware sensor access.

Qué hace la app
