What the App Does

Lumina Core estimates your device's power consumption using a statistical model based on CPU, GPU, and RAM load. It calculates electricity cost projections using a unit price you set. All processing happens locally on your device.

About Accuracy

The power values shown are statistical estimates, not hardware sensor readings. Real consumption may vary depending on your device model, workload, and environment. Use the figures as a practical guide, not as precise measurements.
